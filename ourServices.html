At C J Spurr Builders Warkworth, we understand that the thought of building can be daunting. When you build with us, you can be assured of our expert advice and experience to guide you through the whole house building process from start to finish, from the earthworks right through to final completion and Council signoff.

Communication is key, so we will listen to you and walk you through every stage of the entire building process.

We don’t stretch ourselves between multiple projects; you can be assured you will receive our full attention. All builds are project managed by Chris Spurr personally at C J Spurr Builders. This is our work ethic and we believe that this sets us apart from other house builders.

Over the years, we have built solid relationships and alliances with excellent sub-contractors, suppliers, designers, architects and engineers. This ensures a coordinated approach is taken, so that each project is completed to an exceptional standard.

HOME BUILDERS WARKWORTH HAS GROWN UP WITH

At C J Spurr Builders Warkworth, we have been delivering high quality, personalised residential construction services to our clients for over 25 years. We build strong, long-standing houses and strong, long-standing personal relationships with our homeowners around the greater Rodney region.

C J Spurr Builders Warkworth was established by Director, Chris Spurr 25 years ago. Chris is a fully qualified builder and Licenced Building Practioner and has extensive experience within the building industry.

Chris personally overseas and is hands on with every project, seeing it through from inception to completion. C J Spurr Builders Warkworth is not a housing company, therefore Chris works on every project and personally oversees every detail. He believes in maintaining an open line of communication at all times with every client. There is no middleman, the work is done by the C J Spurr building team, they are responsible for it and proud of it.

C J Spurr Builders specialise in providing an exceptional quality building and customer service to each and every client.

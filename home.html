At C J Spurr Builders Warkworth, we have been delivering high quality, personalised residential construction services to our clients for over 25 years. We build strong, long-standing houses and strong, long-standing personal relationships with our homeowners around the greater Rodney region.

We project manage and build architecturally designed new homes, renovate existing homes and also build outdoor landscaping structures such as decks, cabanas, stairs, and sheds.

As local Rodney builders, we have worked with clients in Warkworth, Algies Bay, Kaipara, Leigh, Mahurangi West, Mangawhai, Matakana, Maungataroto, Omaha, Pakiri, Rainbows End, Riverhead, Sandspit, Scotts Landing, Snells Beach and Tawharanui to name a few places.
